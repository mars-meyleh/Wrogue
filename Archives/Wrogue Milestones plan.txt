Wrogue Milestones plan/lore

The goal is to rebuild the town, currently we (the player) are in this ruins, settlement called Ashroot, we don't know it's story, it's past. We received an invitation letter from the Adventurer Guild's Master Krongar, asking for our aid and any other adventurer available to control the deepths of Ashroot's dungeon.
With the letter the payment from the last job we took from the guild plus a presentation letter to the Guild's Master Varsgo, he is still young and was sent there to gain experience, according to Krongar's gossip on the matter.

Once at the  town we notice how devasted the situation actually is, our job is to occasionally report the situation back to Krongar as a scout to the king's consul, and support the town the best we can, we kill monsters, gather information, construct a codex and help the economy grow. Because of our efforts the King's Consul will give us the town key as a symbol of "you solve the problem now it's your responsability to keep taking care of it". with this we may unlock the new "staates of the city".

And maybe we build a city hall (or the type of building for administrative town business) to deal with the promotions and new quests to go to the next steps.
